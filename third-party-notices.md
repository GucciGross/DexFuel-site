# DexFuel Third-Party Notices

Last updated: July 20, 2026

## Open Food Facts

Some imported product and nutrition information is provided by [Open Food Facts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org). Its database and contents are available under the applicable open-data licenses. Product images, when shown, remain subject to their applicable licenses. DexFuel is not affiliated with or endorsed by Open Food Facts, and users should verify package labels.

## USDA FoodData Central

Some nutrition search results may use [USDA FoodData Central](https://fdc.nal.usda.gov) when that optional server integration is configured. USDA data is informational and can differ from a specific product or prepared portion.

## Apple Health

Apple Health and HealthKit are Apple technologies. DexFuel can request optional access to steps, sleep, resting heart rate, HRV, active energy, cardio fitness, weight, cycling power, and workout context. DexFuel can save a completed workout and recorded route to Apple Health only when the user chooses to do so. Apple does not sponsor or endorse DexFuel.

## Mapbox

Outdoor maps, satellite map layers, route previews, and user-requested offline map regions use the [Mapbox](https://www.mapbox.com) SDK and services. Mapbox may process technical network and map-usage information under its applicable terms and privacy policy. Map imagery is not emergency or satellite-communication coverage.

## WandGX

When cloud Dex AI is enabled and the user allows an online feature, request content is processed through the DexFuel proxy and may be sent to the configured WandGX service at `llm.wandgx.com`. DexFuel's privacy policy describes the applicable context, controls, and retention disclosures.

## RevenueCat

If DexFuel Pro subscriptions are enabled in the submitted build, purchase status and App Store transaction information are processed using [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com). Subscription payments are processed by Apple. DexFuel does not receive full payment-card details.

## Generated Content

Optional AI features use the configured DexFuel AI processor to generate estimates, recipes, workouts, guidance, or representative meal artwork. Generated content can be inaccurate and must be reviewed before use.
