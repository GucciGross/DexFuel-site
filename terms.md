# DexFuel Terms and Health Disclaimer

Last updated: July 20, 2026

DexFuel provides educational nutrition, fitness, habit-tracking, and planning tools. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, medication, peptide dosing, injection instructions, or emergency services.

Nutrition values, photo estimates, generated recipes, and generated workouts can be incomplete or inaccurate. Review food labels, allergen information, exercise instructions, and generated results before relying on them. Stop an exercise if it causes pain, dizziness, or unusual symptoms.

Consult a qualified clinician before materially changing diet or exercise when pregnant, managing a health condition or eating disorder, recovering from injury, taking medication, or following a prescribed treatment plan. For an emergency, contact local emergency services.

Users remain responsible for food safety, allergen avoidance, exercise technique, and decisions made using the app. Optional user-entered peptide schedules are organizational records only and do not replace clinician instructions.

DexFuel may change or discontinue features. Use of third-party nutrition databases and AI processors is also subject to those providers' applicable terms.
